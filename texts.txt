---Interrogative Words---
Wh-questions:
    -interrogative determiner
        which, what
        whose (personal possessive determiner)
    -interrogative pro-form
        -interrogative pronoun
           who, whom, whose (personal)
           what, which
        -interrogative pro-adverb
            where (location)
            when (time)
            how (manner)
                how come (manner of manner)
                how far  (manner of location)
                how long (manner of time)
                how much (manner of quantity)
            why (reason)
            whether and whatsoever (choice between alternatives)
